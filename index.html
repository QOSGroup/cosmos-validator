What we do

We provide a secure and highly available validator infrastructure for the Cosmos Network featuring:

  • Proactive 24/7 monitoring and on-call personnel
  • Secret key material secured by Hardware Security Modules
  • Fully redundant hardware and co-location environment
  • Fully redundant network connectivity with dedicated bandwidth
  • High level of operational automation for scaling and predictability
  • 20+ years of IT operations experience

To completely align our interests with those of our delegators, we will be staking exclusively through our own service.

We have closely followed as well as participated in the development of the Cosmos software since early 2016. This has given us the opportunity to gain extensive operational experience and a deep understanding of the underlying technology.
